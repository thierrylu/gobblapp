Gobbl Beta 0.9 has been developed in collaboration with Nate Ferrero (Back-End Developer), Xiaoye Lin (UI/UX Designer), Thierry Lu (Product Manager) and Synova Solutions (Front-End Developer).

We have built this app to help food lovers better locate their favorite dishes by:

  • collecting and displaying photos of dishes nearby
  • customizing a feed according to their tastebuds
  • allowing them to search for specific dishes
  • showing them what their friends liked

Similarly, this app will allow restaurants owners to better showcase their menu by sharing photos of their best dishes to Gobbl users.

Please feel free to share your feedback on your likes and dislikes of this version by sending an email to feedback@getgobble.com.

Happy Gobbl'in!
